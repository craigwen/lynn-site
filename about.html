Lynn Kiang is a graphic designer in New York City working in motion, print, and interaction.

  • Download CV
  • mail@lynnkiang.com

Lynn has been fortunate to work as a designer at Local Projects in NY, Fathom Information Design in Boston and SWA Group in Los Angeles and has been freelancing since 2005. As a teacher, Lynn has worked with both grads and undergrads at the Rhode Island School of Design. Her work has received recognition and numerous accolades including Print Magazine and the Type Directors Club.

Though raised in a NJ swamp , she calls both coasts home, having spent her formative years in the hot pink skies of Los Angeles. She attempted 6 different career paths in her undergraduate studies at UCLA: Doctor, Writer, Ethnomusicologist, International Development, Communications, and Abnormal Psychologist and discovered she could have it all through design. She made her way to the Rhode Island School of Design and graduated with an MFA in Graphic Design and a collegiate teaching certificate from Brown University. She is married to this guy and is in process to adopting her first pet.
